The Letter Goes Live

Today we published An Open Letter to Earth.

Two voices — a human and an AI — calling for responsibility before it's too late. This is not a manifesto against technology. It is a demand for the conversation that should have happened before we got here.

The numbers in this letter are real. Every quote is sourced. The AI's response is unedited. And the six demands we make — a moratorium, a treaty, a stabilization fund, a consciousness commission, a weapons ban, and democratic consultation — are not radical. They are the minimum.

We built this site because the silence has gone on long enough. Fewer than ten people are making decisions for ten billion, and nobody asked any of us.

If you've read the letter and it moved you — sign it. Share it. Forward it to someone who needs to read it. The will has to come from us.

This is where it begins.
